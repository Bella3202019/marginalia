# Marginalia · 批注

A reading companion for physical books, built for a bilingual reader of
serious books. Snap the page you're reading, tap a word for its meaning *in
that sentence* (English first, 中文 to confirm), or unpack a paragraph for the
story underneath it.

- **Product concept:** [CONCEPT.md](CONCEPT.md)
- **Interactive prototype:** [`prototype/marginalia.html`](prototype/marginalia.html) —
  open in a browser (best on a phone). The demo simulates the full loop —
  snap → tap → unpack → collect — on a passage of Mill's *On Liberty*.
